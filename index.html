DeepImageJ is a user-friendly plugin that enables the use of a variety of pre-trained deep learning models in ImageJ. The plugin bridges the gap between sophisticated between deep learning and standard life-science applications. DeepImageJ runs on a standard CPU-based computer and does not require any DL expertise. The concept is to bundle the TensorFlow model to additional information in order to be use in ImageJ/Fiji as a standard plugin.

A first selection of state-of-the-art models from various groups has been made available in an online database. Beyond its direct use, we expect DeepImageJ to contribute to the spread and validation of DL models in life-science applications.
