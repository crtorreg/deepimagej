Deep ImageJ

Bringing deep learning technology to ImageJ

General description

DeepImageJ is a user-friendly plugin that enables the use of a variety of pre-trained deep learning (DL) models in ImageJ. The plugin acts as a software layer between TensorFlow and ImageJ, hence bridging the gap between sophisticated DL technology and standard life-science applications.

Designed like a typical ImageJ plugin, DeepImageJ runs on a standard CPU-based computer and does not require any DL expertise.

A first selection of state-of-the-art models from various groups has been made available in an online database. Beyond its direct use, we expect DeepImageJ to contribute to the spread and validation of DL models in life-science applications.
